Beginner's Guide - An Introduction

 

Welcome to the Beginner's Guide page! If you are new to Visual Novel Creation, may this page help you out on your endeavour.

 

What is a Visual Novel?

A Visual Novel is an interactive novel or game that features static graphics, usually in Anime/Manga style, music and even occasional Voice Acting. It has minimal gameplay and heavy emphasis towards plot and character(s). They are similar to Choose your own Adventure novels. The main difference is that the narration is often told in the first person point of view.

 

It is important to remember that there are many types of Visual Novels and styles of presentation.It's a very flexible genre where you can add mechanics that found in RPGs, Simulation and other game genres. You can find more information about Visual Novels on the internet and the TVTropes summary is a great place to start.

 

Why Visual Novel Maker?

Most Visual Novel tools are text-based and requires a roundabout way to customize your games. What Visual Novel Maker aims to achieve is to streamline the process and make development faster and retain the freedom of text based engines.

 

How do I create a Visual Novel?

There are many ways in tackling the creation of your Visual Novel, as there is no 'One True Way' in VN development. Some may start with characters and others may start with a more structural approach. If you are experienced in writing or visual novels in general and only want a quick rundown of the process, Aevee Bee created an article where you can make a plan to finish writing in a month and the VN in three and a half months.
