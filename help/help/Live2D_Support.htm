Live2D Support

 

Visual Novel Maker supports Live2D which allows you to use amazing looking animated Live2D characters for your VN!

 

 

Special Agreement

It is not just Live2D, DEGICA Co. Ltd. and Live2D Inc. together have a custom agreement which gives you special possibilities when using Live2D with VN Maker!

 

That special agreement allows you to use Live2D and the Live2D Example Models for your game without any additional license agreements or fees. You don't need to worry about anything! Just use Live2D with VN Maker and then you can publish and even sell your game without any kind of additional license agreements or fees!

 

Enable Live2D Support

Live2D is integrated into VN Maker, but you still need to enable it if you haven't bought the bundled version of VN Maker. You have to download and install the Live2D Support DLC for VN Maker and done!
